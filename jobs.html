Environmental Scientist

Reference: RE439

Salary: $100,000 - $120,000

Reports to: Project Managers

Key Responsibilities

  1. Provide practical, outcome-focused environmental advice to project managers, design teams, and contractors.
  2. Review environmental risks, incidents and performance reports for accuracy and completeness.
  3. Support and contribute to environmental management systems.

Essential Requirements

  • Teritiary Qualification in Environmental Science, Environmental Management, Engineering or equivalent.
  • 3+ years of experience in consultancy (7+ years for Senior Level)
  • Demonstrated experience in and working knowledge on Federal and State environmental legislations.

Preferable Requirements

  • Clear communicator with a collaborative working style.
  • Strong planning, organisation and attention to detail.

Environmental Advisor

Reference: RE528

Salary: $115,000- $125,000

Reports to: Environmental Managers

Key Responsibilities

  1. Monitor compliance with licenses, approvals and regulatory conditions.
  2. Prepare internal reports and external submissions including license returns and annual reports.
  3. Facilitate environmental risk assessments.

Essential Requirements

  • Tertiary qualification in Environmental Science, Environmental Engineering or equivalent.
  • Strong understanding of environmental management systems, monitoring and reporting.
  • Strong technical writing, data management and reporting skills.

Preferable Requirements

  • Familiarity with incident investigations and internal auditing beneficial.
  • Data driven mindset with ability to translate results into action.
